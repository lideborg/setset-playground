## Chanel

### Photography Style
- **Lighting Approach**: Soft, natural lighting; sun-drenched environments; dusk lighting for chromatic portraits; low, subtle illumination that flatters subjects
- **Composition**: Architectural framing; blurred lines between interior and exterior spaces; graphic forms suspended in equilibrium; balanced duality (black-and-white vs. vibrant color)
- **Angles & Framing**: Intimate interior shots; garden settings with architectural elements; heritage locations (Villa Noailles in South of France); mixture of full-length and portrait-style compositions
- **Key Photographers**: Inez & Vinoodh, Steven Meisel, Mikael Jansson

### Color Palette
- **Dominant Colors**: Pastel palette (coral, mint, muted pink); classic black and white; vibrant floral hues
- **Tonal Preferences**: Soft beiges, creams, and whites; occasional bold pops of neon or vivid chromatic colors
- **Color Grading**: Natural, organic color treatment; emphasis on texture visibility; chromatic portraits in soft lighting

### Background/Setting Choices
- **Primary Settings**: French villas and heritage architecture; lush gardens; pink rooms with ornate details; sun-drenched outdoor spaces
- **Studio Work**: Black-and-white studio portraits emphasizing graphic silhouettes
- **Location Examples**: Villa Noailles in Hyères (Spring 2024); Hangzhou for Métiers d'art (2024/25)

### Mood/Atmosphere
- **Overall Feeling**: Relaxed elegance; sophisticated leisure; architectural majesty meeting fashion refinement
- **Emotional Tone**: Timeless luxury; narrative storytelling; celebration of craftsmanship and heritage; feminine confidence
- **Atmosphere**: Sun-filled optimism; romantic yet structured; blend of casual sophistication and high glamour

### Styling Characteristics
- **Clothing Presentation**: Tweed jackets and suits as signature pieces; graphic checks and bold stripes; lush floral prints; crystal-embellished details
- **Model Posing**: Elegant yet relaxed; natural movement in gardens and architectural spaces; poised but approachable
- **Key Styling Elements**: Layered pearls; camellia flower accents; structured silhouettes with soft details; mix of masculine and feminine elements

### Key Visual Markers (AI-Prompt Keywords)
```
- Iconic tweed jacket with structured shoulders and metallic buttons
- Strands of lustrous pearls layered at neck
- White camellia flower detail on lapel or as accessory
- Soft natural daylight through French windows
- Pastel color palette with beige, cream, blush pink
- Ornate architectural molding and heritage interiors
- Lush garden setting with flowering plants
- Black and white checkered pattern
- Quilted leather texture with diamond stitching
- Gold chain strap detail
- Elegant villa setting in South of France
- Sophisticated feminine silhouette with cinched waist
- Classic bourgeois Parisian elegance
```

## Saint Laurent (YSL)

### Photography Style
- **Lighting Approach**: High-contrast black-and-white; dramatic chiaroscuro; moody atmospheric lighting; occasional vibrant color work to contrast monochrome aesthetic
- **Composition**: Strong geometric forms; tableaus inspired by classical paintings (Michelangelo, Caravaggio, Manet); sharp angles and clean lines
- **Angles & Framing**: Both portrait and full-body compositions; art-historical references in framing; angular, architectural approach to body positioning
- **Key Photographers**: Glen Luchford, Gray Sorrenti, Collier Schorr, David Sims, collaborations with artists like Daidō Moriyama

### Color Palette
- **Dominant Colors**: Primarily black-and-white; when color is used: deep blacks, crisp whites, metallic golds, rich reds
- **Tonal Preferences**: Monochromatic with high contrast; dramatic darks and lights
- **Color Grading**: Sharp, crisp contrast; film noir influence; occasional bold color pops

### Background/Setting Choices
- **Primary Settings**: Black studio backgrounds; urban Parisian Left Bank settings; art-historical tableaus; stark minimalist environments
- **Studio Work**: Predominantly black-and-white studio photography; controlled environments emphasizing silhouette
- **Artistic References**: Classical painting compositions; references to 20th-century directors (Visconti, Pasolini)

### Mood/Atmosphere
- **Overall Feeling**: Moody, sexy rock 'n' roll; louche enigma; contemporary playfulness with classical gravitas
- **Emotional Tone**: Sensual sophistication; androgynous allure; rebellious elegance; unabashedly sexy
- **Atmosphere**: Nocturnal mystique; artistic intellectualism; powerful femininity with masculine edge

### Styling Characteristics
- **Clothing Presentation**: Sharp tailoring; sleek leather jackets; iconic Le Smoking tuxedo; utilitarian "Safari" detailing with razor-sharp chic; dramatic evening wear with deep V-cuts
- **Model Posing**: Powerful, confident stances; angular positioning; references to Helmut Newton's provocative style; slouched sophistication
- **Key Styling Elements**: Slicked hair; white cravat with tuxedo; satin lapels; sharp shoulders; floor-length gowns with artful necklines; dramatic draping

### Key Visual Markers (AI-Prompt Keywords)
```
- Sharp-shouldered black tuxedo jacket with satin lapels
- Sleek leather jacket with rock-and-roll edge
- High-contrast black and white photography
- Dramatic moody lighting with deep shadows
- Androgynous model with slicked-back hair
- White dress shirt with black bow tie
- Powerful stance with angular body positioning
- Urban Paris nighttime setting
- Le Smoking tuxedo silhouette
- Dramatic floor-length evening gown with plunging neckline
- Strong cheekbones and defined features
- Minimalist black studio background
- Metallic gold accents on black fabric
- Sensual yet sharp tailoring
- Film noir aesthetic with Helmut Newton influence
```

## Dior

### Photography Style
- **Lighting Approach**: Soft, diffused interior lighting; romantic natural light; baroque-influenced dramatic lighting; emphasis on texture and ornate details
- **Composition**: Interior shots with intricate architectural detail; Renaissance and baroque painting influences; layered depth with wallpaper, molding, and fabric
- **Angles & Framing**: Impeccably composed interior shots; full-length portraits in ornate settings; frames-within-frames using architectural elements
- **Key Photographers**: Brigitte Niedermair, Sarah Jones, Julia Hetta, Elina Kechicheva, Margot Populaire (creative director)

### Color Palette
- **Dominant Colors**: Soft pastels (lilac, peacock blue, pistachio green, blush pink); classic navy and white; rich jewel tones
- **Tonal Preferences**: Delicate feminine palette; baroque richness; toile de jouy blues and reds on cream
- **Color Grading**: Romantic, slightly vintage-inspired; emphasis on fabric texture and pattern

### Background/Setting Choices
- **Primary Settings**: Mysterious apartment interiors; ornate Parisian rooms with period details; baroque and Renaissance-inspired environments
- **Key Elements**: Blue velvet curtains; leopard-print accents; ornate banisters and molding; floral wallpaper; toile de jouy patterns; crystal chandeliers
- **Studio Work**: Minimal studio work; preference for richly decorated interiors

### Mood/Atmosphere
- **Overall Feeling**: Romantic femininity; baroque opulence; pop couture meeting classical elegance; mysterious yet inviting
- **Emotional Tone**: Sophisticated glamour; Parisian elegance; celebration of craftsmanship and feminine power; literary and artistic references
- **Atmosphere**: Dreamlike luxury; elegant mystery; Renaissance-meets-modern; refined theatricality

### Styling Characteristics
- **Clothing Presentation**: Bar jacket with cinched waist and peplum; intricate embroidery and embellishment; lace and delicate fabrics; XXL graffiti-style logos; sculptural silhouettes
- **Model Posing**: Graceful, feminine poses; thoughtful, contemplative expressions; elegant movement within ornate interiors; young women captured in intimate moments
- **Key Styling Elements**: Crystal embellishments; ruffle details; floral lace; toile de jouy patterns; Miss Dior logo; structured shoulders with feminine waist

### Key Visual Markers (AI-Prompt Keywords)
```
- Bar jacket with nipped waist and peplum hem
- Toile de jouy pattern in classic blue on cream background
- Ornate baroque interior with velvet curtains
- Delicate lace overlay on structured silhouette
- Soft romantic lighting through period windows
- Blue velvet draping and luxurious fabric
- Ornate gilded molding and architectural details
- Floral embroidery with crystal embellishments
- Pastel color palette with blush pink and soft blue
- Sculptural feminine silhouette with full skirt
- Period Parisian apartment with decorative wallpaper
- Renaissance painting composition and framing
- Feminine graceful pose in elegant interior
- Mixed patterns: florals, toile, leopard print
- Sophisticated craftsmanship details visible in close-up
```

## Hermès

### Photography Style
- **Lighting Approach**: Clean, minimalist lighting; natural light emphasizing craftsmanship; soft shadows highlighting texture; product-focused illumination
- **Composition**: Flemish still-life painting inspiration; hero angles showcasing details; expansive natural settings balanced with close-up craftsmanship shots
- **Angles & Framing**: Close-ups of stitching and hardware; full product shots; headshot portraits for beauty campaigns; architectural precision
- **Key Photographers**: Craig McDean, Julien Martinez Leclerc, Agnes Lloyd-Platt, Harry Gruyaert

### Color Palette
- **Dominant Colors**: Iconic orange (Orange H, Feu, Orange Poppy, Orange Minium, Potiron); neutral beiges and browns; rich leather tones
- **Tonal Preferences**: Earthy, natural palette; black and white with orange accents; warm cognac and saddle brown
- **Color Grading**: Natural, showing true material colors; emphasis on leather patina and texture; clean color separation

### Background/Setting Choices
- **Primary Settings**: Clean minimalist backgrounds; urban elegance (Paris to New York); equestrian environments; natural outdoor settings
- **Studio Work**: Simple, sophisticated studio portraits; beige and brown backdrops featuring iconic leatherwork
- **Environmental**: Lush blooming gardens; architectural mosaics; references to Faubourg Saint-Honoré flagship

### Mood/Atmosphere
- **Overall Feeling**: Timeless craftsmanship; quiet luxury; refined elegance; equestrian heritage
- **Emotional Tone**: Confident sophistication; artisan precision; exclusivity without ostentation; understated power
- **Atmosphere**: Serene luxury; dedication to craft; heritage and innovation; sophisticated restraint

### Styling Characteristics
- **Clothing Presentation**: Equestrian-inspired tailoring; clean silhouettes; focus on accessories (bags, scarves); minimal styling to showcase product quality
- **Model Posing**: Elegant, timeless poses; emphasis on product interaction; sophisticated restraint; natural, confident bearing
- **Key Styling Elements**: Silk carré scarves (twilly); Birkin and Kelly bags; saddle leather details; equestrian references; hand-rolled scarf edges

### Key Visual Markers (AI-Prompt Keywords)
```
- Iconic orange branded box and packaging
- Hand-stitched saddle leather with visible craftsmanship
- Silk scarf (carré) with intricate printed design
- Structured leather handbag with metallic hardware
- Equestrian-inspired bridle and saddle details
- Clean minimalist composition with product focus
- Beige and cognac leather tones
- Orange accent color as signature element
- Close-up of hand-rolled silk scarf edge
- Architectural precision in product photography
- Timeless elegant silhouette without trend-chasing
- Neutral palette emphasizing material quality
- Artisan craftsmanship details in macro
- Equestrian heritage references
- Understated luxury and refined restraint
```

## Celine (under Hedi Slimane)

### Photography Style
- **Lighting Approach**: Black-and-white photography with dramatic contrast; raw, unpolished aesthetic; natural lighting creating authentic documentary feel
- **Composition**: Still-life minimalism; portrait-focused; documentary style; indie rock concert energy; urban youth culture framing
- **Angles & Framing**: Direct, unflinching portraits; minimal background distraction; focus on individual subjects and couples; 1960s photography influence
- **Key Photographers**: Hedi Slimane himself (creative and image director); influenced by Daidō Moriyama's black-and-white work

### Color Palette
- **Dominant Colors**: Primarily black-and-white; when color appears: muted tones, vintage film colors
- **Tonal Preferences**: High contrast monochrome; grainy texture; raw documentary aesthetic
- **Color Grading**: Film photography grain; authentic, unretouched feel; vintage rock photography aesthetic

### Background/Setting Choices
- **Primary Settings**: Paris locations; club culture environments; minimalist studios; urban settings; concert venues
- **Aesthetic**: Documentary realism; youth culture scenes; indie music environment; stripped-back authenticity
- **Cultural Context**: Music culture submersion; portrait series of cultural contributors; nightlife documentation

### Mood/Atmosphere
- **Overall Feeling**: Haunting minimalism; indie rock melancholy; androgynous allure; raw youth energy
- **Emotional Tone**: Rebellious sophistication; nostalgic coolness; authentic subcultural documentation; rock 'n' roll spirit
- **Atmosphere**: Paris nightlife; punk-rock rawness; glam androgyny; postpunk attitude; skinny-suited New Wave aesthetic

### Styling Characteristics
- **Clothing Presentation**: Ultra-slim tailoring; skinny silhouettes; androgynous styling; leather jackets; narrow trousers; rock-chic elements
- **Model Posing**: Natural, candid poses; youth culture authenticity; minimal styling direction; indie musician aesthetic; raw performance energy
- **Key Styling Elements**: Skinny jeans; slim-cut suits; leather details; androgynous layering; vintage rock t-shirts; minimal accessories

## Notes on Usage

### For AI Image Generation:
- Avoid using brand names directly; instead combine multiple descriptive keywords
- Layer technical photography terms with styling descriptions
- Reference art movements and photographic styles rather than brand names
- Combine setting, lighting, color, and mood descriptors for best results

### Brand Distinction Strategy:
- **Chanel** = Natural light + pastels + heritage architecture + tweed texture
- **Saint Laurent** = Black/white contrast + sharp angles + nocturnal mood + leather
- **Dior** = Baroque interiors + romantic pastels + ornate details + lace
- **Hermès** = Minimalist + orange accent + leather craftsmanship + equestrian
- **Celine** = Monochrome + slim silhouettes + youth culture + documentary raw

### Photographic Period References:
- **Chanel**: Contemporary editorial (2020s), soft commercial
- **Saint Laurent**: 1970s Helmut Newton, film noir, classical art
- **Dior**: Renaissance painting, baroque portraiture, romantic 18th century
- **Hermès**: Flemish still-life, mid-century product photography
- **Celine**: 1960s rock photography, punk documentation, raw concert photos
