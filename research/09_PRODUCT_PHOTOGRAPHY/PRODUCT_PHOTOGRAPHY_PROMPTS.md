# Product Photography Prompt Guide

**For AI-Generated High-End Fashion Product Photography**

---

## Shot Type Overview

### 1. Flat Lay
- **Camera:** Bird's-eye view at 90-degree overhead angle
- **Lens:** 50mm prime lens perspective (avoids edge distortion)
- **Surface:** White marble, natural oak wood, textured linen
- **Lighting:** Soft diffused overhead lighting eliminates shadows
- **Composition:** Rule of thirds, intentional negative space
- **Best for:** Complete outfit stories, accessories, editorial styling

### 2. Ghost Mannequin
- **Technique:** Invisible mannequin showing 3D garment structure
- **Key elements:** Interior collar/neck visible, shoulder structure maintained
- **Background:** Pure white seamless
- **Lighting:** High-key studio softbox, shadowless
- **Composition:** Centered, fills 85% of frame
- **Best for:** Structured garments (shirts, blazers, dresses)

### 3. Hanger Photography
- **Hanger types:** Polished walnut wood, velvet-covered padded, satin
- **Lighting:** Soft natural window light from left
- **Background:** White wall, textured neutral
- **Key:** Natural drape shows fabric weight, slightly angled view
- **Best for:** Showing natural drape, casual/lifestyle aesthetic

### 4. Styled/Editorial
- **Approach:** Narrative-driven, magazine-worthy composition
- **Props:** Complementary accessories, lifestyle context
- **Lighting:** Dramatic directional or soft natural
- **Key:** Each element serves the story, intentional negative space
- **Best for:** Campaign imagery, social media, editorial content

## Technical Specifications

### Aspect Ratios
- **1:1 (Square):** Industry standard for Amazon, Shopify
- **2:3 (Vertical):** Fashion favorite, ideal for clothing
- **3:4 (Portrait):** Good for taller products
- **4:5 (Instagram):** Mobile-first platforms

### Resolution
- **Minimum:** 1000px (enables zoom)
- **Recommended:** 1600-2048px
- **Optimal file size:** 100-300KB for web performance

### Camera Settings
- **Aperture:** f/8 for product sharpness throughout
- **ISO:** 100 for clean, noise-free images
- **Focus:** Tack-sharp on product details

## Language That Conveys Luxury

### Material Descriptors
- "Premium," "impeccable craftsmanship," "artisanal"
- "Supple leather," "sumptuous cashmere," "lustrous silk"
- "Heritage quality," "investment piece"

### Avoid
- "Photorealistic" (creates digitized appearance)
- Brand names (copyright issues)
- Vague terms ("nice," "good," "beautiful")

## Category-Specific Notes

### Tops/Shirts
- **Flat lay:** Sleeves positioned to show natural drape
- **Ghost:** Collar stands naturally, interior label visible
- **Hanger:** Sleeves fall naturally, shows fabric weight

### Sweaters/Knitwear
- **Flat lay:** Neatly folded showing texture and weave pattern
- **Ghost:** Shoulder structure preserved, texture detail sharp
- **Hanger:** Velvet padded hanger, shows knit weight

### Outerwear
- **Flat lay:** Lapels and collar positioned naturally, lining visible
- **Ghost:** Structured shoulders maintain tailored shape
- **Hanger:** Full length from shoulder to hem

### Dresses
- **Flat lay:** Complete silhouette from neckline to hem
- **Ghost:** Fitted bodice shows silhouette, skirt drapes naturally
- **Hanger:** Padded satin hanger, fabric flows with gravity

### Bottoms
- **Flat lay:** Full length from waistband to hem, hardware visible
- **Ghost:** Waistband structure clear, leg line visible
- **Hanger:** Chrome clip hanger, natural drape

### Shoes
- **Display:** Pair at complementary angles (straight + 45Â°)
- **Details:** Sole visible, stitching detail, hardware catches light
- **Styled:** Marble pedestal, dust bag for luxury context

### Bags
- **Display:** Front three-quarter angle showing dimension
- **Details:** Hardware (clasps, zippers, feet) visible
- **Styled:** Velvet surface, complementary accessories

### Accessories
- **Surface:** White marble or black velvet
- **Lighting:** Precision lighting for material detail
- **Styled:** Curated arrangement, museum-quality presentation
