# Super Minimal Backgrounds + Editorial Poses System

**Key Principles:**
1. Backgrounds must be SUPER clean and minimal (no clutter, no detail)
2. Poses must feel editorial (attitude, angles, fashion-forward)

---

## Background Rules: SUPER MINIMAL ONLY

### ✅ ALLOWED (Clean, Empty, Minimal):
- White seamless paper
- Concrete floor/wall (plain)
- White walls (empty)
- Salt flats (nothing but white)
- Desert (empty sand)
- White sky (fog/overcast making everything white)
- Minimal geometric structures (single wall, single line)
- Empty plaza/platform (nothing on it)

### ❌ NOT ALLOWED (Too Busy):
- ~~Lush greenery~~
- ~~Botanical conservatory~~
- ~~Forest clearing~~ (too much detail)
- ~~Glass architecture~~ (too much visual interest)
- ~~Circular windows~~ (too much detail)
- ~~Steps with railings~~
- ~~Gravel gardens with stones~~

---

## Editorial Poses: Fashion-Forward Minimal

### Tier 1: Classic Editorial (Use Most)
- **Hands on hips** - powerful, confident
- **Arms crossed** - strong, editorial attitude
- **One hand in pocket** - casual confident
- **Hand on hip weight shifted** - asymmetrical, dynamic
- **Leaning against wall** - relaxed editorial
- **Arms at sides** - neutral powerful

### Tier 2: Angled Editorial (Add Variety)
- **Profile turned away** - side view, looking off
- **Three-quarter turn** - not facing camera directly
- **Looking over shoulder** - turned away, glance back
- **Walking away** - motion, back view
- **Sitting on edge** - casual seated

### Tier 3: Advanced Editorial (Use Sparingly)
- **One leg crossed in front** - standing asymmetrical
- **Hand touching face/chin** - contemplative editorial
- **Arms behind back** - open stance
- **Looking down** - introspective
- **Head tilted** - intimate
