# Brand Renaming Strategy

Based on comprehensive research of luxury, avant-garde, and beauty brand aesthetics, we're renaming our 7 aesthetic profiles to generic names while refining their visual characteristics.

---

## Renaming Map

| Current Name | New Name | Research Inspiration |
|-------------|----------|---------------------|
| The Row | **Quiet Luxury** | The Row, Jil Sander, Lemaire |
| Haute Couture | **French Elegance** | Chanel, Dior, Hermès |
| Rick Owens | **Dark Avant-Garde** | Rick Owens, Yohji Yamamoto, Comme des Garçons |
| Lululemon | **Athletic** | Premium activewear aesthetic |
| Acne Studios | **Scandinavian** | Nordic minimalism |
| Stüssy | **Streetwear** | Contemporary street culture |
| Glossier | **Fresh Beauty** | Glossier, Charlotte Tilbury, Fenty |

---

## Refined Aesthetic Profiles

### 1. QUIET LUXURY
**Inspiration:** The Row, Jil Sander, Lemaire, Bottega Veneta

**Visual Characteristics:**
- Ultra-refined minimalism with extreme restraint
- Neutral monochromatic palette (cream, camel, slate, charcoal, ivory)
- Soft natural lighting or high-contrast B&W
- Plain backgrounds with zero distraction
- Film photography warmth
- Sculptural silhouettes and architectural precision
- Elevated realism

**Key Descriptors (NO BRAND NAMES):**
- quiet luxury, refined minimalism, understated elegance
- monochromatic sophistication, architectural precision
- soft natural light, analog warmth, film photography aesthetic
- neutral tones, sculptural silhouettes, elevated realism

---

### 2. FRENCH ELEGANCE
**Inspiration:** Chanel, Dior, Hermès, Saint Laurent

**Visual Characteristics:**
- Soft, romantic lighting in heritage locations
- Baroque interiors or French gardens
- Pastel palettes mixed with classic black/beige
- Timeless sophistication with ornate details
- Feminine sculptural silhouettes
- Equestrian or artisan craftsmanship details

**Key Descriptors (NO BRAND NAMES):**
- French elegance, Parisian sophistication, timeless refinement
- baroque interiors, heritage gardens, romantic architecture
- soft natural light, pastel tones, classic neutrals
- ornate details, feminine silhouettes, artisan craftsmanship

---

### 3. DARK AVANT-GARDE
**Inspiration:** Rick Owens, Yohji Yamamoto, Comme des Garçons, Maison Margiela

**Visual Characteristics:**
- Brutalist, monochromatic aesthetic
- High-contrast black & white or all-black
- Dystopian, apocalyptic mood
- Desert, industrial, or stark concrete settings
- Dramatic chiaroscuro lighting
- Geometric, architectural compositions
- Grunge-glamour contradiction

**Key Descriptors (NO BRAND NAMES):**
- dark avant-garde, brutalist architecture, dystopian mood
- monochromatic brutalism, apocalyptic aesthetic, industrial minimal
- high-contrast lighting, dramatic shadows, geometric compositions
- raw concrete, desert expanse, urban grit

---

### 4. ATHLETIC
**Inspiration:** Premium activewear and wellness brands

**Visual Characteristics:**
- Clean, bright wellness aesthetic
- Natural daylight or studio softbox lighting
- Vibrant but refined color palette
- Minimal backgrounds (white studio or outdoor nature)
- Active, powerful poses showing movement
- Gender-specific color coding (men: dark/neutral, women: pastels/brights)

**Key Descriptors (NO BRAND NAMES):**
- athletic wellness, active lifestyle, fitness elegance
- bright clean aesthetic, natural movement, powerful presence
- vibrant refined colors, minimal backgrounds, studio lighting
- activewear sophistication, wellness culture

---

### 5. SCANDINAVIAN
**Inspiration:** Nordic minimalism, Acne Studios aesthetic

**Visual Characteristics:**
- Cool, cerebral minimalism
- Muted earth tones with black/navy/charcoal
- Clean lines and oversized silhouettes
- Architectural urban settings or minimal studios
- Natural diffused lighting
- Edgy but restrained aesthetic

**Key Descriptors (NO BRAND NAMES):**
- Scandinavian minimalism, Nordic cool, cerebral aesthetic
- muted earth tones, architectural urban settings, clean lines
- oversized silhouettes, natural diffused light, restrained edge
- cool sophistication, minimal urban

---

### 6. STREETWEAR
**Inspiration:** Contemporary California street culture

**Visual Characteristics:**
- Casual, authentic street aesthetic
- Vibrant saturated colors or neutral basics
- Urban settings (streets, skate parks, concrete)
- Natural daylight or golden hour lighting
- Relaxed, confident poses
- High-contrast color grading

**Key Descriptors (NO BRAND NAMES):**
- streetwear culture, California casual, urban authentic
- saturated colors, street photography, concrete urban settings
- natural daylight, golden hour warmth, relaxed confidence
- surf culture, skate aesthetic, vibrant casual

---

### 7. FRESH BEAUTY
**Inspiration:** Glossier, Charlotte Tilbury, Fenty Beauty, La Mer

**Visual Characteristics:**
- Skincare-focused with emphasis on dewy, glowing skin
- Close-up beauty portraits (f/2.8 aperture)
- Three lighting variations:
  - 1/3: Regular bright/soft
  - 1/3: Warm golden/honey light
  - 1/3: Wet/dewy skin texture emphasis
- White or minimal backgrounds
- Soft pink/cream/pearl color palette
- Fresh, intimate, natural beauty

**Key Descriptors (NO BRAND NAMES):**
- fresh beauty, skincare glow, dewy radiant skin
- beauty closeup, glowing skin texture, natural radiance
- soft pink tones, warm honey light, wet dewy texture
- intimate beauty, minimal clean aesthetic, skin-first

---

## Implementation Rules

1. **NEVER use brand names in prompts** (no The Row, Chanel, Rick Owens, etc.)
2. **Use aesthetic descriptors only** (quiet luxury, French elegance, brutalist, etc.)
3. **Maintain gender-specific clothing** where appropriate
4. **Keep research-based visual accuracy** (lighting, color palettes, backgrounds)
5. **Avoid logo generation** by using generic aesthetic terms
